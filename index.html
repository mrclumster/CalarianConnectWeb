The Citizen App Interface

The Everyday Impact

Transforming hours of waiting into minutes of processing.

The Old Way

  • Citizens wait 5-10 minutes just for manual record lookups.
  • Forms are filled by hand and physical logbooks are used.
  • Paper requests get lost with no status visibility.
  • Hours of queuing for simple certificates and clearances.

The Calarian Way

  • Walk-ins are identified in 2-3 seconds via Face ID.
  • Documents are auto-generated to PDF and printed instantly.
  • Citizens get live push notifications on their phone.
  • Requests submitted from home take minutes to process.

Bank-Grade Privacy

Your data is safe. We don't save pictures of your face, and only authorized staff can update your records.

Tech Detail

Compliant with the Data Privacy Act (RA 10173). Faces are stored as 512-dimension mathematical vectors in PostgreSQL (pgvector), making reverse-engineering a photo impossible. Database-level Row Level Security (RLS) ensures citizens only see their own data.
